Events

CSI M.ART.IN brings to you a variety of technical and non-technical events. Read on to learn more!

Detective GITAMite

The participants are required to solve a crime mystery with the help of some clues which will be hidden in the campus area. This is a team game consisting of maximum of 5 members. The team which solves the mystery in the minimum amount of time wins

Ad Hog

In this event, the participants will be shown a part of an advertisement or a mixture of different advertisements and the participants need to guess them correctly.

Dub It Up

The event consists of participants using trending applications like Musical.ly and Dubsmash to create dubsmashes on the theme of ‘College Diaries’.

Pic-O-Mania

In this event, a team should consisting of three will be given information about five objects and they should search for the objects in the campuswhich they have to find and take a selfie with it and submit it to the organizers. The team which finds all the objects in minimum amount of time wins.

Guess What

This is a team game and each team should consist of two members. A word will be given to one of the members. He/she should give a single worded hint to the other member and the latter should guess the word.

Freak-O-Matic

An individual event, where the participants will be given a questionnaire of maths puzzles. Participant who solves the maximum number of questions in minimum amount of time wins

Moviewood Hunt

In this event, either an individual or a team of two members can participate. In the beginning of the game, the participants will be asked to choose among Bollywood (Hindi Movies), Hollywood (English Movies) and Tollywood (Telugu Movies). Depending on their choice, a variety of questions will be asked.

Lost?

An event which tests the participants memory to the fullest. Initially, the participants are shown a pattern to remember. The participants then have to traverse through a darkened room according to the pattern. Participant who goes the furthest in the least amount of time wins.

Wordsmiths

An innovative writing event which tests the contestant’s creativity under a stipulated time.

Code-a-thon

A two-part marathon of MCQs based on missing code followed by a programming challenge based on real-life situation.

Technical Quiz

A hustle of non-programming quiz where questions are based on trending software discoveries, technical current affairs and innovations.

Bug Bounty

A debugging event in which code snippets of varying difficulties in different languages to be solved within a limited time-period.

Crack-und-Solve

A multi round contest with rounds varying from puzzles to coding questions to questions on current affairs.

Just One

An event which consists of questions which have to be solved in Python only. The participants must solve the question using only one line of python code.

Trust Me

An event where participants need to trust each other to solve semantical and syntactical questions in pairs.
